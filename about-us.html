About Us

Home About Us

Providing a service to the construction, Petrochemicaln Mining, Civil Engineering and relation industries - Nationwide

Shuma Steel Works excel in both soecialist and industrial steel fabrication. Construction services for practically any conventional steel or pre-engineering building structure.

We strive to maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty and fairness in our relationships with our clients, suppliers, business partners and professional associates.

Our Vision

To be one of the leading, successful emerging Black-Owned business in the Steel Industry.

Our Mission

Shuma Steel Works' mission is to give clients the highest level of quality services at fair and current market related prices and to ensure the longevity of the company through repeat and referral business achieved by client satisfaction in all areas including deadlines, attention to detail and service-minded attitudes.

Our Safety Policy

Management considers no phase of operations or administration of greater importance than accident prevention, and asserts that accidents which result in personal injury and damage to property and equipment, represent needless waste and loss.

Shuma Steel Works' basic objective is to establish throughout the entire organisation the firm and full concept that people and property are our most valuable resource.

Our very existence is based upon this principle.

Our Quality Policy

  • Exceed customers needs and expectations in offering the best products and services
  • Recognize and develop all employees’ contributions to continuous improvement
  • Promote an effective Quality Management System
  • Management communicate, commit and involve all personnel to a total quality drive
